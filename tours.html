Karbach Brewing Co. is centrally located right outside of the 290/610 interchange near the intersection of Mangum and Dacoma.

2032 Karbach Street, Houston TX 77092

Mondays 4:30-7:30PM (Tour at 6PM)
Thursdays 4:30-8:30PM (Tour at 6PM)
Fridays 4:30-7:30PM (Tour at 5:15 and 6:15PM)
Saturdays 12-3PM (Tour at 1 and 2:15PM)

Tour space is limited to 25 people (first come, first served- no reservations). Sign up for tours upon check in. Closed-toed shoes are required

$8.00 admission includes:

  • 9oz Tour Glass
  • 3 "Classic" Token (Hopadillo IPA, Weisse Versa Wheat, Sympathy for the Lager, Weekend Warrior Pale Ale, El Hopadillo Negro and Seasonal)
  • 1 "Specialty" Token (Rodeo Clown DIPA, Rotating Specialty, Cask)

$12.00 admission includes:

  • 16 oz pint glass
  • 2 "Classic" Token (Hopadillo IPA, Weisse Versa Wheat, Sympathy for the Lager, Weekend Warrior Pale Ale, El Hopadillo Negro and Seasonal)
  • 1 "Specialty" Token (Rodeo Clown DIPA, Rotating Specialty, Cask)

Patrons bringing their own Karbach pint glass (16 oz) will receive $1 off the $12 admission price. Patrons bringing a canned food donation will receive $1 off the admission price. Beer is also available by the glass for the following:

  • $2 - 9 oz "Classic"
  • $4 - 9 oz "Specialty"
  • $3 - 16 oz "Classic"
  • $5 - 16 oz "Specialty
  • $5 - 9 oz F.U.N. Series (when available)

* Own glass needed for by-the-glass fills

Note: Karbach only fills 9 oz or 16 oz glasses during tasting/tour hours. Other size glasses are available in the gift shop, but are souvenirs and will not be filled.

Private Events

Thank you for your interest in hosting an event at Karbach! Our event space is located outdoors in our shaded biergarten. We strongly urge you to visit the brewery before booking your event to see the facility. Please feel free to contact us if you have any questions or to arrange a walk through.

Tuesday or Wednesday Rates: $15 per person ($1000 minimum charge)

Saturday or Sunday: $15 per person ($2000 minimum charge)

Are you a 501c3 looking to host a fundraiser? Click here for more information!

The Biergarten is available for a 3 hour rental during the following hours:

  • Tuesday and Wednesday: 5-9pm
  • Saturday: 5-9pm
  • Sunday: All Day

Brewery Rental Rate Includes:

  • 2 bartenders and private biergarten (3 bartenders for 150+ guests).
  • Core and Seasonal beers on tap.
  • All guests will receive a Karbach Brewing Co. logo pint glass or tasting glass to use and take home. The amount of glasses given out will determine headcount and we will charge accordingly.
  • Custom souvenir glassware can be purchased prior to the event at a bulk rate. Please allow at least 4 weeks for order processing.

Other Important Information:

  • A $500 non-refundable deposit is required to hold/reserve a party date.
  • The balance due is to be paid 48 hours prior to the start of the event.
  • The minimum rental amount will be applied to the first 65 guests on Tuesday/Wednesday and the first 135 guests on Saturday/Sunday. Any additional guests will be charged at the conclusion of the event.
  • Beer is unlimited for the agreed timeframe or a specific number of drink tokens may be designated.
  • Private catering is strongly encouraged.
  • Nonalcoholic beverages are not supplied, please bring your own water and soda.
  • NO OUTSIDE ALCOHOL IS PERMITTED.
  • Absolutely NO drinking games (Flip cup, beer pong, beer bongs, etc.).
  • 18% gratuity will be added to the final amount.

For scheduling and other questions please contact: events@karbachbrewing.com
